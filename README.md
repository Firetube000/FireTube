# Firetube Documentation

## Overview
Firetube is a powerful video hosting platform that allows users to upload, share, and manage their videos effectively. Whether you are a creator looking to reach a broader audience or a viewer who loves discovering new content, Firetube has everything you need.

## Features
- **User-friendly interface**: Navigate easily through the platform.
- **Upload Videos**: Simple process to upload your videos in various formats.
- **Video Management**: Edit and organize your videos with playlists and categories.
- **Community Engagement**: Comment, like, and share videos, and connect with other users.
- **Analytics**: Access detailed analytics for your uploaded videos to understand viewer engagement.

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up**: Create an account on Firetube by providing your basic details.
2. **Profile Setup**: Customize your profile to showcase your personality.
3. **Upload Your First Video**: Follow the upload guidelines to share your content.
4. **Explore**: Check out content from various creators to inspire your own.

## Technologies Used
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express
- **Frontend**: React
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Cloud Storage**: AWS S3 for video storage
